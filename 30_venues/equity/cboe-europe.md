# Cboe Europe

**Cboe Europe Equities** — pan-European MTF group (post-merger of BATS Europe and Chi-X Europe). Operates lit, dark, and periodic-auction books across UK and EU.

## Asset classes

- European equities (across all major EU and UK listings)
- ETFs
- ETPs

## Workflow mechanisms

- **CXE (Cboe Lit)** — CLOB book.
- **BXE (Cboe Periodic Auction)** — discrete-time auction book.
- **Cboe LIS (Block)** — large-in-scale block matching.
- **CCP**-linked clearing via EuroCCP (Cboe-owned).

## Connectivity

- **BOE (binary)** — Cboe's proprietary low-latency.
- **FIX 4.2 / 4.4** for institutional.
- Market data via Cboe Europe feed.

## Key facts

- Significant share of European lit and periodic-auction volume.
- Periodic auctions are particularly important as a MiFID II workaround for the double-volume cap on dark trading.
- EuroCCP integration provides clearing across Cboe Europe + competing venues.
