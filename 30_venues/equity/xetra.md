# Xetra (Deutsche Boerse)

**Deutsche Boerse**'s primary cash-equity venue — Xetra is the **CLOB platform** for DAX, MDAX, SDAX, EURO STOXX 50, and other German/European listings. Deutsche Boerse also operates **Eurex** for derivatives separately.

## Asset classes

- German-listed equities (DAX, MDAX, SDAX)
- EURO STOXX 50 component cross-listings
- ETFs (Xetra is one of the largest ETF venues in Europe)

## Workflow mechanisms

- **CLOB** with opening / midday / closing auctions.
- **Continuous trading** between auctions.
- **MiFID II compliant** with EU RTS 1 transparency.

## Connectivity

- **T7** — Deutsche Boerse's unified low-latency platform (binary).
- **FIX 4.2 / 4.4** institutional.
- Market data via Deutsche Boerse feeds.

## Key facts

- The largest German lit equity venue.
- ETF franchise particularly strong (most European ETFs trade Xetra).
- T7 is shared with Eurex — operationally a single tech stack for the EU complex.
