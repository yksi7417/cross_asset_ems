# 360T (Deutsche Boerse)

Deutsche Boerse Group's **multi-bank FX trading platform**, headquartered in Frankfurt. Strong in **EUR-pair flow, corporate treasury, and short-end money market / FX swap** trading.

## Asset classes

- FX spot (all majors and most EM)
- FX outrights, swaps
- FX NDFs (selected)
- FX options
- Money market deposits
- Precious metals

## Workflow mechanisms

- **RFQ** to dealer set.
- **RFS (Request-for-Stream)**.
- **Continuous price streaming** with click-to-trade.
- **Algorithmic execution** for selected pairs / strategies.

## Connectivity

- **FIX 4.4 / 5.0** for order entry, RFQ, executions.
- REST / WebSocket APIs for pricing and reference.
- Deutsche Boerse market data integration.

## Key facts

- Particularly strong in **corporate treasury** flow — see [[fxel]].
- Headquartered in EU, FCA-/BaFin-regulated MTF.
- Distinct algo offering for spot / NDF.
