# Bloomberg Bridge

Bloomberg's **anonymous, intermediated all-to-all** electronic trading service for **emerging-market and corporate bonds**. Buy-side participants trade with each other (and dealers) anonymously, with Bloomberg as the intermediating counterparty for matched trades.

## Asset classes

- Emerging-market hard-currency bonds (sovereigns, corporates)
- Selected USD / EUR / GBP IG and HY corporate
- Some local-currency EM bonds

## Workflow mechanisms

- **All-to-all anonymous matching** — buy-side responds to other buy-sides' inquiries without identity disclosure.
- **Bloomberg as central counterparty** for matched trades (anonymity intermediation).
- **Live inquiry distribution** with permissioned audience selection.

## Connectivity

- **FIX** for order entry and ExecutionReports.
- BLPAPI for reference and post-trade.
- Integration with Bloomberg's broader FI platform (FIT, ALLQ data feeds in).

## Key facts

- Directly competes with MarketAxess Open Trading in the all-to-all space.
- Particularly strong in EM bonds where dealer balance sheet has retreated.
- Pre-trade analytics (CBBT, BVAL) embedded.
