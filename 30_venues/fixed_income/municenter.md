# MuniCenter (Tradeweb Direct)

Tradeweb's **electronic platform for US municipal bonds** (and CDs). Formerly operated as "The MuniCenter," now integrated into the Tradeweb Direct retail ATS family.

## Asset classes

- US municipal bonds (GO, revenue, refunding, BABs)
- Certificates of deposit (CDs)
- Treasuries (retail-sized)
- Agency bonds

## Workflow mechanisms

- **Dealer-streamed firm prices** with click-to-trade.
- Retail / wealth-management focused — supports odd-lot and round-lot.
- Integrates with the broader Tradeweb Direct retail platform.

## Connectivity

- **FIX** for order routing.
- REST API for inventory snapshots.
- MSRB RTRS reporting integrated post-trade.

## Key facts

- One of the longer-running electronic muni venues.
- Distinct from the institutional muni flow on MarketAxess.
