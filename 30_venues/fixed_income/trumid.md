# Trumid

US-headquartered **electronic credit trading venue**, focused on **all-to-all anonymous matching** for US IG and HY corporate bonds. Direct competitor to MarketAxess Open Trading and [[bloomberg-bridge]].

## Asset classes

- US IG corporate bonds
- US HY corporate bonds
- Emerging-market hard-currency bonds (growing)

## Workflow mechanisms

- **Attributed Trading (AT)** — disclosed dealer RFQ.
- **Anonymous all-to-all matching** through the central pool.
- **Trumid PT (Portfolio Trading)** for basket execution.
- **Swarms** — concentrated matching events on specific bonds.

## Connectivity

- **FIX** for order entry, RFQ, executions.
- REST API for inventory and reference.

## Key facts

- Differentiated by its anonymity and the "Swarms" mechanism that concentrates liquidity.
- Significant growth in HY all-to-all share post-2020.
- Used in combination with MarketAxess by most large buy-side firms (not either-or).
