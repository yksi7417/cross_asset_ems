# Euronext

**Pan-European exchange group** with primary listing venues in Paris, Amsterdam, Brussels, Lisbon, Dublin, Oslo, and Milan (Borsa Italiana joined in 2021). Single Optiq trading platform across all member exchanges.

## Asset classes

- European equities (CAC 40, AEX, BEL 20, ISEQ, OBX, PSI 20, FTSE MIB)
- ETFs (multi-listing footprint)
- ETPs
- Fixed income (smaller — separate venue)

## Workflow mechanisms

- **CLOB** with opening / closing auctions per market.
- **Continuous trading** between auctions.
- **Optiq** — unified order-entry across all Euronext markets.

## Connectivity

- **Optiq Binary** — low-latency proprietary protocol.
- **FIX 4.2 / 4.4** institutional.
- Market data via Euronext OptiqMDG.

## Key facts

- The largest pan-European exchange group by market cap.
- Single tech stack across all venues simplifies multi-market routing.
- Borsa Italiana addition (2021) brought FTSE MIB and MTS sister.
