# ICE BondPoint

ICE Group's **odd-lot fixed-income ATS**, focused on **retail-sized US corporate, municipal, agency, and CD inventory**. SEC-registered ATS. Recently part of the ICE Fixed Income platform alongside ICE Credit Trade and TMC Bonds.

## Asset classes

- US IG and HY corporate bonds (odd-lot focus)
- US municipal bonds (significant share of retail muni flow)
- US agency bonds
- Certificates of deposit (CDs)
- Treasuries (limited)

## Workflow mechanisms

- **Order-driven / quote-driven** with dealer-streamed firm prices.
- **Click-to-trade** on dealer quotes.
- Optimized for the **odd-lot retail / private-bank** use case where RFQ workflows are too heavy.

## Connectivity

- **FIX** for order routing and execution reports.
- REST APIs for reference data and inventory.

## Key facts

- BondPoint historically dominant in retail muni inventory distribution.
- Sister venues under ICE Fixed Income: ICE Credit Trade (institutional), TMC Bonds.
