# Yieldbroker

ASX-owned electronic trading platform for **Australian and New Zealand rates and credit**. Dominant in AUD govts (ACGB, semis), AUD IRS, NZ govts, and AUD/NZD repo. D2C and D2D segments.

## Asset classes

- Australian Commonwealth govt bonds (ACGBs)
- Semi-government bonds (NSW, QLD, VIC, etc.)
- New Zealand govt bonds (NZGBs)
- AUD / NZD IRS, OIS, basis swaps
- AUD bills (BBSW-linked) and short-end
- AUD / NZD repo

## Workflow mechanisms

- **RFQ** D2C for govt and credit.
- **CLOB / order-book** for D2D ACGB lines.
- Integrated post-trade reporting to RBA/ASIC where applicable.

## Connectivity

- **FIX** for order entry, RFQ, allocations.
- REST API for reference data.

## Key facts

- Acquired by ASX in 2021 — anchoring the AUD electronic rates franchise.
- The dominant electronic venue for AUD govt and IRS D2C.
