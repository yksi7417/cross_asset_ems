# LSE (London Stock Exchange)

**London Stock Exchange Group** (LSEG)'s **primary UK listing exchange**. Operates the SETS (CLOB) order book for FTSE 100/250 names and SETSqx for less-liquid lines. Sister venues across LSEG: Borsa Italiana, Tradeweb (FI), MTS (FI), Curve Global (historically).

## Asset classes

- UK-listed equities (FTSE 100, 250, AIM)
- ETFs (UK-domiciled and dual-listed)
- Selected DRs (depositary receipts)
- Selected fixed income (retail bond market — separate from MTS)

## Workflow mechanisms

- **SETS** — CLOB with opening / closing auctions.
- **SETSqx** — periodic auctions + market-maker quotes for less-liquid.
- **MiFID II compliant** post-Brexit (LSE operates as a UK RIE).

## Connectivity

- **MIT (Millennium Information Technology)** — proprietary binary low-latency.
- **FIX 4.2 / 4.4** for institutional.
- Market data via LSEG Real Time Data.

## Key facts

- The dominant UK lit equity venue; competition from Cboe Europe and Aquis.
- Closing auction is the largest UK single liquidity event.
- Cross-listed with US (ADRs) for many FTSE 100 names.
