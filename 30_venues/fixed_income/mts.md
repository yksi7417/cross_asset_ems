# MTS

Italian-headquartered (London Stock Exchange Group) **European fixed-income trading platforms**. MTS is the dominant venue for **European government bond interdealer trading** and a major D2C platform for credit and EU repo.

## Asset classes

- European govt bonds — BTP, Bund, OAT, SPGB, Bonos, Gilts, etc.
- EU repo (MTS Repo)
- EU corporate bonds (MTS BondVision D2C and BondsPro)
- Cash management / money markets

## Workflow mechanisms

- **CLOB** for interdealer govts (MTS Cash).
- **D2C RFQ** for govts and credit (BondVision).
- **Repo CLOB and term repo RFQ** (MTS Repo).
- **Auction support** for EU sovereign issuance.

## Connectivity

- **FIX** for order entry, RFQ, executions.
- Proprietary low-latency binary protocol for CLOB.
- REST API for reference and post-trade.

## Key facts

- The dominant venue for BTP, Bund, OAT interdealer flow.
- Selected as primary auction support for several EU debt management offices.
- Sister venues to LSEG's broader fixed-income strategy (Tradeweb is also LSEG).
